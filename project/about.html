Our History & Owners

Before it became a premier hub for sports enthusiasts, the site was a quiet residential area. The center was built with the vision of bringing the community together through sports and recreation.

Owned and operated by a dedicated team of sports lovers, our mission is to provide top-notch facilities and services to promote a healthy lifestyle in Cyberjaya for the students who need to blow off steam after long study sessions.

Why Choose Us?

We are not just a sports hall, we are a community hub designed for serious athletes.

  1. Digital-First Booking: No more phone calls. Book and pay for your court in under 60 seconds through our system.
  2. Student Special: We offer the best rates in Cyberjaya specifically for MMU students with valid IDs.
  3. Professional Grade Surfaces: Our courts use Olympic-standard flooring to prevent injuries and improve game quality.
  4. Strategic Location: Located right in the heart of the tech city, making us the perfect spot for after-class matches.
