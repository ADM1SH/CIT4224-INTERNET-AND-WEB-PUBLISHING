Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I book a court?

A: You can book a court by registering as a member, logging in, and selecting your desired slot from the Our Courts page.

Q: What are the peak and off-peak hours?

A: Peak hours are from 5:00 PM to 12:00 AM daily. Off-peak hours are from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM on weekdays.

Q: Can I cancel my booking?

A: Yes, you can cancel your booking through the dashboard at least 2 days before the scheduled time for a refund.

Q: Is there any equipment for rent?

A: Yes, we provide badminton rackets and futsal balls for a small rental fee at the reception counter.
